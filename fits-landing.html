FITS combines your wardrobe preferences, body measurements, and personal style into complete, shoppable outfits. Mix pieces from different brands, swap items in a single tap, and check out with your entire look in one place — no tabs, no confusion, no compromise.

How it works
01

Build your profile

02

Describe what you need — a job interview, a Sunday brunch, a winter weekend look, or anything in between. FITS understands context.

What makes FITS different

Fashion that knows you, shopping that just works.

True personalisation

FITS learns your proportions, your preferred brands, your style references, and your budget. Every suggestion is filtered through your specific context — not generic trends.

One cart, many brands

Shop H&M, Zara, ASOS, Mango — all in a single checkout. FITS handles the cross-brand complexity so you don't have to.

Shop for the whole family

Create sub-profiles for your kids, partner, or parents. Each profile gets its own personalised outfits — all ordered from one account, on one device.

Complete looks, zero effort

Stop buying clothes you love but never wear together. FITS assembles the entire outfit — top, bottom, shoes, bag — into one cohesive look, ready to buy.
